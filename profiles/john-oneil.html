Dr. john O'Neil

Consultant Neurologist

Dr. John O'Neil is a Consultant Neurologist with over 20 years of clinical experience in the diagnosis and management of complex neurological conditions. He specialises in headaches and migraine disorders, epilepsy, neurodegenerative diseases, and nerve conduction disorders. Dr. O'Neil completed his medical degree at the University of Edinburgh, followed by specialist neurology training at major teaching hospitals in the UK and Ireland. He has a strong interest in patient‑centred care and is known for his calm, thorough, and compassionate approach. Throughout his career, he has contributed to several clinical research projects involving early‑stage Parkinson’s disease and has presented at multiple national neurology conferences. He continues to engage in ongoing professional development and evidence‑based practice. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, Dr. O'Neil provides comprehensive assessment, diagnosis, and personalised treatment plans for patients of all ages with neurological concerns.

Special Interests:

  • Headache & migraine management
  • Epilepsy
  • Parkinsons & movement disorders
  • Peripheral nerve disorders
  • Cognitive decline & memory assessment

Experience:

20+ years in neurology across public and private healthcare settings.
