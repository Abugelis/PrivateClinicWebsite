Consultant Gynaecologist

Dr. Franklin Saint is a Consultant Gynaecologist with over 17 years of experience in women’s health, specialising in reproductive endocrinology, minimally invasive surgery, and prenatal care. He is dedicated to providing compassionate, patient-focused care, helping women at all stages of life achieve optimal health outcomes. Dr. Saint completed his medical degree at University College Cork and underwent specialist gynaecology training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. He has contributed to research in fertility preservation and endometriosis management and regularly presents at national conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, Dr. Saint offers personalised consultations, advanced diagnostic procedures, and tailored treatment plans for patients across a wide range of gynaecological conditions.

Special Interests:

  • Fertility & reproductive medicine
  • Endometriosis & pelvic pain management
  • Minimally invasive gynaecological surgery
  • Prenatal & antenatal care
  • Menopause management

Experience:

11+ years in gynaecology across public and private healthcare settings.
