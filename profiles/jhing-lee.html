Dr. Jhing Lee

Consultant Dermatologist

Dr. Jhing Lee is a Consultant Dermatologist with over 17 years of experience in diagnosing and managing a broad spectrum of skin conditions. She specialises in acne, eczema, psoriasis, mole checks, and non-invasive cosmetic dermatology procedures. Dr. Lee earned her medical degree at University College Dublin and completed her dermatology training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. She is known for her meticulous approach, clear patient communication, and dedication to evidence-based treatments. Throughout her career, Dr. Lee has contributed to dermatology research projects and has presented at national and international dermatology conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, she provides comprehensive skin assessments, personalised treatment plans, and guidance on preventive skin care.

Special Interests:

  • Acne & eczema management
  • Psoriasis treatment
  • Skin cancer screening & mole assessment
  • Cosmetic dermatology (laser, chemical peels)
  • Pediatric dermatology

Experience:

17+ years in dermatology across public and private healthcare settings.
