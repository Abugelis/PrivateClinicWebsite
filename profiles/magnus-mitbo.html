Dr. Magnus Mitbo

Consultant Oncologist – Radiotherapy

Dr. Magnus Mitbo is a Consultant Oncologist specialising in radiotherapy, with over 20 years of experience in the treatment of cancer patients. He is skilled in planning and delivering precise radiotherapy protocols, working closely with multidisciplinary teams to ensure optimal outcomes. Dr. Mitbo earned his medical degree at Trinity College Dublin and completed his specialist oncology and radiotherapy training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. He is recognised for his compassionate, patient-centred approach and dedication to advancing evidence-based cancer care. Throughout his career, Dr. Mitbo has contributed to clinical research in radiotherapy techniques for breast, lung, and prostate cancers and has presented at numerous national and international oncology conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, he provides personalised treatment plans, advanced radiotherapy options, and ongoing support for patients undergoing cancer therapy.

Special Interests:

  • Radiotherapy for solid tumours (breast, lung, prostate)
  • Multidisciplinary cancer care
  • Oncology patient counselling
  • Clinical research in radiotherapy
  • Symptom management and palliative care

Experience:

20+ years in oncology and radiotherapy across public and private healthcare settings.
