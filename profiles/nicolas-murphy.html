Dr. Nicolas Murphy

Consultant Neurologist

Dr. Nicolas Murphy is a Consultant Neurologist with over 22 years of experience in diagnosing and treating complex neurological conditions. He specialises in epilepsy, neurodegenerative disorders, headaches and migraines, and peripheral nerve disorders. Dr. Murphy earned his medical degree at University College Dublin and completed his neurology training at major teaching hospitals in Ireland and the UK. He is known for his patient-centred approach, thorough assessments, and clear communication with patients and their families. Throughout his career, Dr. Murphy has contributed to clinical research in early-stage Parkinson’s disease and multiple sclerosis and has presented at national neurology conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, he provides comprehensive neurological assessments, personalised treatment plans, and ongoing care for patients of all ages.

Special Interests:

  • Epilepsy management
  • Headache & migraine treatment
  • Neurodegenerative disorders (Parkinson’s, Alzheimer’s)
  • Peripheral nerve disorders
  • Cognitive and memory assessments

Experience:

22+ years in neurology across public and private healthcare settings.
