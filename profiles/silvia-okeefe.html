Dr. Silvia O'Keefe

Consultant Radiologist

Dr. Silvia O’Keefe is a Consultant Radiologist with over 18 years of experience specialising in diagnostic imaging, including MRI, CT, ultrasound, and X-ray. She has a strong focus on accurate diagnostics and patient safety, providing detailed interpretations to support effective treatment planning. Dr. O’Keefe earned her medical degree at University College Dublin and completed her radiology training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. She is recognised for her meticulous approach and clear communication with both patients and multidisciplinary medical teams. Throughout her career, Dr. O’Keefe has contributed to research in advanced imaging techniques and regularly presents at national radiology conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, she provides precise diagnostic imaging, interpretation, and collaboration with other specialists to ensure optimal patient care.

Special Interests:

  • MRI & CT imaging
  • Ultrasound diagnostics
  • X-ray and radiographic interpretation
  • Interventional radiology procedures
  • Oncology imaging

Experience:

18+ years in radiology across public and private healthcare settings.
