Dr. Grace Ryan

Consultant Radiologist

Dr. Grace Ryan is a Consultant Radiologist with over 16 years of experience in diagnostic imaging and interventional radiology. She specialises in MRI, CT scans, ultrasound, and image-guided procedures for a wide range of medical conditions. Dr. Ryan earned her medical degree at University College Cork and completed her radiology training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. She is known for her precision, attention to detail, and ability to explain complex imaging results clearly to both patients and referring physicians. Throughout her career, Dr. Ryan has contributed to research on advanced imaging techniques and has presented at national radiology conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, she provides accurate diagnostics, personalised imaging reports, and collaborative care alongside consulting specialists.

Special Interests:

  • MRI & CT imaging
  • Ultrasound diagnostics
  • Breast & musculoskeletal imaging
  • Interventional radiology procedures
  • Clinical research in imaging techniques

Experience:

16+ years in radiology across public and private healthcare settings.
