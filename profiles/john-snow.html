Dr. John Snow

Consultant Dermatologist

Dr. John Snow is a Consultant Dermatologist with over 19 years of experience in diagnosing and treating a wide range of skin conditions. He specialises in acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin cancer screening, and cosmetic dermatology. Dr. Snow earned his medical degree at Trinity College Dublin and completed his dermatology training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. He is recognised for his patient-centred approach, attention to detail, and dedication to providing clear guidance and treatment options for his patients. Throughout his career, Dr. Snow has contributed to clinical research in skin cancer prevention and advanced dermatological treatments and has presented at multiple national dermatology conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, he offers comprehensive skin assessments, personalised treatment plans, and ongoing support for dermatological health.

Special Interests:

  • Acne & eczema management
  • Psoriasis care
  • Skin cancer screening & treatment
  • Cosmetic dermatology procedures
  • Pediatric dermatology
