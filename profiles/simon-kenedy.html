Dr. Simon Kenedy

Consultant Oncologist – Radiotherapy

Dr. Simon Kenedy is a Consultant Oncologist specialising in radiotherapy, with over 18 years of experience in treating a wide range of cancers. He focuses on delivering precise radiotherapy treatments while ensuring compassionate, patient-focused care. Dr. Kenedy earned his medical degree at University College Dublin and completed his oncology and radiotherapy training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. He is known for his meticulous planning, collaborative approach with multidisciplinary teams, and clear communication with patients and families. Throughout his career, Dr. Kenedy has participated in clinical research on radiotherapy techniques and has presented findings at multiple national and international oncology conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, he provides personalised treatment plans, advanced radiotherapy options, and continuous support for patients undergoing cancer care.

Special Interests:

  • Radiotherapy for solid tumours (lung, head & neck, prostate)
  • Multidisciplinary cancer care
  • Clinical research in radiotherapy
  • Oncology patient counselling
  • Symptom management and palliative care

Experience:

18+ years in oncology and radiotherapy across public and private healthcare settings.
