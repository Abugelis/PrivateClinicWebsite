Dr. James May

Consultant Gastroenterologist

Dr. James May is a Consultant Gastroenterologist with over 20 years of experience in diagnosing and managing gastrointestinal disorders. He specialises in endoscopic procedures, liver disease, inflammatory bowel disease, and digestive tract cancers. Dr. May earned his medical degree at Trinity College Dublin and completed his gastroenterology training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. He is known for his meticulous approach, clear communication, and dedication to providing personalised patient care. Throughout his career, Dr. May has contributed to clinical research in gastroenterology, particularly in Crohn’s disease and colorectal cancer screening, and has presented his work at national and international conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, he offers comprehensive assessments, diagnostic procedures, and tailored treatment plans for patients with gastrointestinal conditions.

Special Interests:

  • Inflammatory bowel disease (Crohn’s disease, ulcerative colitis)
  • Liver disease & hepatitis management
  • Endoscopic procedures (colonoscopy, gastroscopy)
  • Irritable bowel syndrome & functional GI disorders
  • Nutrition & digestive health guidance

Experience:

7+ years in gastroenterology across public and private healthcare settings.
