Dr. Sean Cormac

Consultant Gastroenterologist

Dr. Sean Cormac is a Consultant Gastroenterologist with over 22 years of experience in the diagnosis and management of digestive disorders. He specialises in inflammatory bowel disease (IBD), liver disease, endoscopic procedures, and general gastrointestinal health. Dr. Cormac completed his medical degree at University College Dublin and undertook advanced gastroenterology training at leading hospitals in Ireland and the UK. He is committed to providing evidence-based, patient-centred care and is known for his thorough approach and clear communication. Throughout his career, Dr. Cormac has contributed to clinical research in liver disease and endoscopy techniques, presenting at several national conferences. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, he offers comprehensive consultations, diagnostic procedures, and personalised management plans for patients with gastrointestinal conditions.

Special Interests:

  • Inflammatory bowel disease (Crohn’s disease, ulcerative colitis)
  • Liver disease & hepatitis management
  • Endoscopic procedures (colonoscopy, gastroscopy)
  • Irritable bowel syndrome & functional GI disorders
  • Nutrition & digestive health guidance

Experience:

22+ years in gastroenterology across public and private healthcare settings
