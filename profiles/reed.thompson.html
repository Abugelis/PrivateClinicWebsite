Dr. Reed Thompson

Consultant Neurologist

Dr. Reed Thompson is a Consultant Neurologist with over 19 years of experience in diagnosing and managing complex neurological disorders. He specialises in multiple sclerosis, neurodegenerative diseases, stroke care, and neurorehabilitation. Dr. Thompson earned his medical degree at the University of Glasgow and completed his neurology training at leading hospitals in the UK and Ireland. He is highly regarded for his patient-focused approach, combining clinical expertise with clear, compassionate communication. Throughout his career, Dr. Thompson has contributed to clinical trials in multiple sclerosis and published research on stroke rehabilitation techniques. At Blue Cross Private Clinic, he provides comprehensive neurological assessments, personalised treatment plans, and ongoing care for patients with both chronic and acute neurological conditions.

Special Interests:

  • Multiple sclerosis & autoimmune neurological disorders
  • Stroke diagnosis and rehabilitation
  • Neurodegenerative diseases (Parkinson’s, Alzheimer’s)
  • Neuropathy & nerve disorders
  • Cognitive assessment & memory disorders

Experience:

19+ years in neurology across public and private healthcare settings.
