Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Blue Cross Private Clinic (“we”, “our”, or “the Website”) uses cookies and similar technologies. This Website is a demonstration/portfolio project and does not track real patient data. Cookies are only used for basic functionality and analytics purposes.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function correctly, improve performance, and provide analytics about site usage.

2. Types of cookies we use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Website to function, such as navigation, security, and loading basic content. They do not store personally identifiable information.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect anonymised information about how visitors use the site, such as pages viewed, time spent, and browser/device data. This information helps us improve the Website’s design and performance.

Analytics cookies may be provided by third-party services such as Google Analytics, depending on your configuration. Any such data is anonymised or aggregated.

2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies help remember basic preferences (e.g., cookie consent selection). They are optional and only active if you give consent.

3. Third-party cookies

Some content on this Website may be provided by third parties (such as maps or embedded tools). These services may use their own cookies according to their privacy and cookie policies.

4. Managing cookies

You can control or disable cookies in several ways:

  • Through your browser’s privacy settings.
  • By clearing your stored cookies.
  • By blocking or restricting specific types of cookies.

If you disable essential cookies, some features of the Website may not function properly.

5. Data collected through cookies

Cookies may collect non-personal technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages viewed. This data is used only for site analytics, security, and performance.

6. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
